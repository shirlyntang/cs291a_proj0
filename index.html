Llamas!!!

Fun facts about llamas:
  • They spit or stick out their tongue when they're annoyed
  • Llamas sometimes communicate by humming
  • Llamas are pretty smart


Here is a table of some different types of llamas in Minecraft:

Name Image Description
Llama The original llama, which comes in 4 possible colors: white, brown, grey, cream
Trader Llama Minecraft traders always spawn with two leashed trader llamas
Baby Llama All llamas spawn as baby llamas
